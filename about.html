Who We Are

Redefining Bathroom Experiences

Bathera is a modern bathware brand focused on creating premium ceramic sanitary products that combine design, durability, and innovation. We believe that every bathroom deserves the same level of attention to detail as the finest spaces in a home.

Founded on the principles of precision manufacturing and design excellence, Bathera has grown into a trusted name for architects, interior designers, and homeowners who demand the best. Our state-of-the-art production facility combines traditional ceramic expertise with modern automation.
